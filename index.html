Our Story

The founding principles of the Big Mountain Club lie in community and connections. This club was founded because it was just so hard to find other people to do hard objectives with. We wanted to change that and we have. Our groupMe is a thriving chat that allows members to find great partners for whatever mission they may have in mind.

Through this community we strive to bring together those who desire epic adventures. From multi-day road trips to ultra marathons to huge mountaineering objectives, we want to challenge ourselves and make great memories. We are a community of adventurers who provide the perfect environment to plan trips, make friends, and talk shop.
